Satoru Gojo

Coldest Character in Jujutsu Kaisen

Satoru Gojo (五ご条じょう悟さとる Gojō Satoru) is one of the protagonists of Jujutsu Kaisen. He is a teacher at Tokyo Metropolitan Curse Technical College.

Appearance

  • Satoru Gojo is a tall man, standing over 190 centimeters.
  • He has white hair that's often spiked up, but Gojo lets it down when he's in more casual attire.
